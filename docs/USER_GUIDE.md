# Lionheart Platform User Guide

## Overview
This guide helps end users navigate and use the Lionheart school operations platform.

### Getting Started
- Sign up or sign in using your school subdomain.
- Dashboard: View requests, events, and reports.
- Settings: Manage your profile, school info, roles, teams, and campus.

### Common Tasks
- Submit a ticket or event request from the dashboard.
- Approve or review tasks in Quick Links.
- Update your profile and school information in Settings.

### Accessibility
- Keyboard navigation is supported.
- Touch targets are sized for mobile.

### Help & Support
- For issues, contact your school admin or IT support team.

---

## FAQ
- How do I reset my password?
  - Use the "Forgot password" link on the sign-in page.
- How do I join a new school?
  - Contact your admin for an invite link.
